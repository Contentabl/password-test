Your Diets

Paleo The diet:
A whole foods diet, comprised of clean meats, organic fruits & vegetables, pastured eggs & some nuts and seeds.
Recommended for:
weight loss, general health management
Whole 30 The diet:
Jump start your weight loss with this 30 day program based on Paleo principles.
Recommended for:
dietary restart, weight loss
Weston Price / Nourishing Traditions The diet:
Traditional, nutrient-dense foods like ferments, offal & sprouted grains comprise this diet. Lots of vegetables and fruits as well as raw milk products, grass-fed meats & pastured eggs.
Recommended for:
families, health maintenance, general diet
GAPS The diet:
Created to fix what's known as "leaky gut", this multi-phase diet is lauded for its healing effects on autoimmune and digestive diseases. The GAPS diet has anecdotal accounts of reversing food allergies, healing the lining of the gut and easing the symptoms of severe gastrointestinal issues. Not for the faint of heart, this diet requires a strong commitment and patience.
Recommended for:
seriously damaged guts, those who are seeking to alleviate leaky gut issues
Primal The diet:
Based on Paleo concepts, Primal includes raw or grassfed organic dairy.
Recommended for:
weight loss, general health management
Low FODMAPs The diet:
Often used for management of Irritable Bowel Disorder. Low in a variety of carbohydrates proven to cause gastrointestinal discomfort, this is a great solution for managing a sensitive gut.
Recommended for:
intestinal-related disorders, IBD, Crohn's, Colitis
Autoimmune Paleo The diet:
A low-allergen approach to Paleo, with an initial phase of no seeds, nuts, eggs, and nightshades. If you have found a plateau in managing or healing your illness with Paleo, Autoimmune Paleo, or AIP, is a great place to start.
Recommended for:
controlling autoimmune disease-related flare-ups, autoimmune disease management, thyroid management, allergy-free weight loss
Wahls Paleo The diet:
An alternative, extremely nutrient-dense approach to autoimmune illness. This version of Paleo subtracts eggs, adds in fermented food & offal, and 9 servings of fruits and vegetables per day.
Recommended for:
autoimmune issues, especially but not limited to those affecting the joints, nerve and muscles, such as Rheumatoid Arthritis, Multiple Sclerosis, Neuropathy & Fibromyalgia
Ketogenic / Bulletproof The diet:
High fat, low carbohydrate, moderate protein. This diet employs the principles of Dave Asprey. This diet puts your body into a state in which fat is burned for energy, versus carbohydrates, resulting in fat loss.
Recommended for:
weight loss
21 Day Sugar Detox The diet:
A three a week program designed to conquer sugar addiction. Based on Paleo principles, but overall sugar intake--including those from fruits and vegetables.
Recommended for:
weight loss, those aiming to cut sugar issues
Gluten-Free The diet:
This diet includes a variety of foods, but is free of all grains containing gluten.
Recommended for:
Celiac disease, Crohn's disease
Vegan The diet:
No animal products whatsoever-- just fruits, vegetables, nuts, legumes & grains.

Vegetarian The diet:
No meat, but eggs and dairy are permissible. Besides eggs and dairy, this diet is comprised of fruits, vegetables, nuts, legumes & grains.

Specific Carbohydrate Diet The diet:
A relatively restrictive diet for people with gastrointestinal disease. Low in specific naturally-occuring carbohydrates, this diet is good for those managing digestive issues, but requires a level of commitment.
Recommended for:
Celiac disease, Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis, diverticulitis
